DIRECTOR MESSAGE

PROF. DEEPENDRA SINGH

DIRECTOR
RAJKIYA ENGINEERING MIRZAPUR

Dear Students, Alumni, Faculties, and Parents,

I am delighted to welcome you to our esteemed institution, Rajkiya Engineering College Mainpuri. As the Director, it is my honor to lead this vibrant community of learners, innovators, and achievers.

At Rajkiya Engineering College Mainpuri, we believe in the transformative power of education and the immense possibilities that lie within each and every student. Our mission is to provide a holistic learning experience that combines academic excellence with practical skills, personal growth, and ethical values. We strive to nurture future engineers who are not only technically proficient but also socially responsible leaders.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, and a highly qualified faculty, we offer a conducive environment for academic exploration and research. Our innovative curriculum is designed to bridge the gap between theory and practice, preparing our students to tackle real-world challenges head-on. We encourage interdisciplinary collaborations, entrepreneurship, and industry exposure to foster a spirit of innovation and creativity among our students.

Beyond academics, we place great emphasis on extracurricular activities and overall personality development. Our clubs, societies, and sports teams provide ample opportunities for students to pursue their passions and discover their hidden talents. We believe in nurturing well-rounded individuals who can excel not only in their chosen fields but also as responsible citizens of society.

Whether you are a prospective student, a parent, Alumni or a member of our faculty, we look forward to your association with Rajkiya Engineering College Mainpuri. Together, let us embark on a journey of knowledge, innovation, and excellence.

Warm regards,

Prof (Dr.) Deependra Singh
Director, Rajkiya Engineering College Mainpuri
