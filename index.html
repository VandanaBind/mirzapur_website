Welcome To RECM

Rajkiya Engineering College, Mirzapur has been recently established by the Government of Uttar Pradesh in the year 2023 offering 4-year undergraduate B.Tech. programme in four disciplines, viz Computer Science and Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics Engineering and Electrical Engineering with annual intake of 60 in each discipline. The B.Tech. course in all four disciplines is affiliated to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow.
During its first academic session 2023-24, the college will operate from the fully residential campus of its mentor institution, Rajkiya Engineering College, Sonbhadra. The 30 acres campus of Rajkiya Engineering College, Sonbhadra comprises of an administrative block, two academic blocks, a workshop, separate hostels for boys and girls with cooperative mess facility, student activity centre, canteen, health centre, open gymnasium, round the clock electricity and water supply and other facilities for sports and recreation.
